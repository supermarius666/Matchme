Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and following the instructions. You will be asked to enter some personal data and your initial preferences.

Is MatchMe free?

MatchMe offers a free basic version with all the essential features to start finding matches. Premium subscriptions with advanced features are also available for an improved experience.

How does the matching system work?

Our matching algorithm uses the preferences you specified in your profile, along with in-app behavior data, to suggest compatible profiles. The more information you provide, the better the suggestions!

Can I report a user?

Yes, the safety of our community is a priority. You can report any inappropriate or suspicious behavior directly from the user's profile or by contacting us via this form.

What is the "Boost" feature?

The "Boost" feature allows you to highlight your profile for a limited period, increasing its visibility and chances of matching.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your profile settings, select "Account" and then "Delete account". Remember that deletion is irreversible.
